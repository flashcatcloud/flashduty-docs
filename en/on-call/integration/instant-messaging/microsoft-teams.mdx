<Warning>
Microsoft Teams integration is currently in Beta stage. The following steps must be completed by a Microsoft Teams administrator.
</Warning>

## 1. Install and Update App

<Steps>
<Step title="Download App">
Download [FlashdutyBot-v1.0.3.zip](https://flashduty-docs.oss-cn-beijing.aliyuncs.com/docs/FlashdutyBot-v1.0.3.zip) to your local machine.
</Step>

<Step title="Install App">
Go to Microsoft Teams, navigate to +Apps → Manage your apps → Upload an app → **Upload an app to your org's app catalog**, then upload the app package `FlashdutyBot.zip`.

![2025-09-18-13-48-04](https://docs-cdn.flashcat.cloud/images/png/bfa4afaf489c3582e858cad99eb76ae5.png)
</Step>

<Step title="Configure App Visibility">
Go to [Microsoft Teams Admin Center](https://admin.teams.microsoft.com/policies/manage-apps), find the Flashduty On-call app, adjust app visibility to everyone or your specified scope.

<Tip>
If the app status shows "Blocked", please wait a moment and refresh the page or modify manually.
</Tip>

![2025-09-18-13-49-11](https://docs-cdn.flashcat.cloud/images/png/ea961e7965a2d0c69f8575c4252fb333.png)
</Step>

<Step title="Verify Installation">
Wait a few minutes, organization members can find this app in +Apps → **Built for your org**.

![2025-09-18-17-05-37](https://docs-cdn.flashcat.cloud/images/png/f7827638d1877005ca674cbbd2aaa4a3.png)
</Step>
</Steps>

### Update App

<Warning>
If your installed app version is lower than 1.0.3, please follow the process below to update.
</Warning>

<Steps>
<Step title="Enter Admin Center">
Go to [Microsoft Teams Admin Center](https://admin.teams.microsoft.com/policies/manage-apps), find and enter the Flashduty app details page.

![2025-09-18-13-50-41](https://docs-cdn.flashcat.cloud/images/png/32a62878f9e765cd8b2eb79ceb00bb02.png)
</Step>

<Step title="Upload New Version">
Upload the new `FlashdutyBot.zip`.

![2025-09-18-13-51-56](https://docs-cdn.flashcat.cloud/images/png/f18ac3504516750c31f8357e65f6d680.png)
</Step>

<Step title="Wait for Update">
Wait for the app version to update in the client (may take tens of minutes).

![2025-09-18-13-52-56](https://docs-cdn.flashcat.cloud/images/png/290f609c30055031ab0ccf6636cdbb01.png)
</Step>
</Steps>

## 2. Link Team

<Steps>
<Step title="Find App">
Find the Flashduty On-call app in the app marketplace.

<Note>
If app not found, please contact your Microsoft Teams organization administrator.
</Note>

![2025-09-18-17-06-34](https://docs-cdn.flashcat.cloud/images/png/0905e663241ce448a1381ef8c08aa777.png)
</Step>

<Step title="Add to Team">
Add the app to the target Team.

<Warning>
This step must select the target Team's General Channel, otherwise incidents cannot be sent to the Team.
</Warning>

![2025-09-18-17-11-29](https://docs-cdn.flashcat.cloud/images/png/01fa86b63d01d2735aa6c4a53efb3c69.png)
</Step>

<Step title="Send Link Command">
In the Team, @Flashduty and send command `linkTeam {ID}`, then click **Link Now**.

![2025-09-18-13-55-05](https://docs-cdn.flashcat.cloud/images/png/3192b5481b0595fcb58e5cc43abad125.png)
</Step>
</Steps>

## 3. Link Chat

<Steps>
<Step title="Find App">
Find the Flashduty On-call app in the app marketplace.

<Note>
If app not found, please contact your Microsoft Teams organization administrator.
</Note>

![2025-09-18-17-06-34](https://docs-cdn.flashcat.cloud/images/png/0905e663241ce448a1381ef8c08aa777.png)
</Step>

<Step title="Add to Chat">
Add the app to the target Chat.

![2025-09-18-17-14-23](https://docs-cdn.flashcat.cloud/images/png/6e56d7de341737fe495e5ff18eb1af34.png)
</Step>

<Step title="Send Link Command">
In the Chat, @Flashduty and send command `linkChat {ID} {ChatName}`, then click **Link Now**.

![2025-09-18-13-56-17](https://docs-cdn.flashcat.cloud/images/png/d0beee141db63714ccecb095affee79b.png)
</Step>
</Steps>

## 4. Link User

<Steps>
<Step title="Find App">
Find the Flashduty On-call app in the app marketplace.

<Note>
If app not found, please contact your Microsoft Teams organization administrator.
</Note>

![2025-09-18-17-06-34](https://docs-cdn.flashcat.cloud/images/png/0905e663241ce448a1381ef8c08aa777.png)
</Step>

<Step title="Open App">
Click **Open App**.

![2025-09-18-13-56-55](https://docs-cdn.flashcat.cloud/images/png/2e6862103d718a913d2b3c449cbf2366.png)
</Step>

<Step title="Send Link Command">
Copy and send command `linkUser {}` to the chat, then click **Link Now**.

![2025-09-18-13-57-13](https://docs-cdn.flashcat.cloud/images/png/671ae7883bbba839419e539762db99de.png)
</Step>
</Steps>

## 5. FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="Team or individual not receiving messages?">
Go to Integration Center → Instant Messaging → **Microsoft Teams**, check if teams and users have been successfully linked.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How to view linked teams and users?">
Go to Integration Center → Instant Messaging → **Microsoft Teams**, view in **Linked Teams** and **Linked Users**.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How to unlink teams and users?">
This feature is not currently supported.
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
