## What is On-Call?

Flashduty On-Call is a unified intelligent alert response platform designed for DevOps, SRE, and operations teams. Through intelligent noise reduction, flexible assignment, and multi-channel notifications, it helps you reduce alert noise by over 90%.

<Note>
On-Call is Flashduty's core product, serving thousands of enterprises and processing over 1 billion alert events.
</Note>

## Workflow

The Flashduty On-Call workflow is intuitive:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Alert Ingestion">
    Alerts are pushed from monitoring systems to Flashduty through integrations
  </Step>
  <Step title="Routing & Assignment">
    Alerts are assigned to the corresponding channel based on routing rules
  </Step>
  <Step title="Intelligent Noise Reduction">
    The system automatically performs alert grouping, noise reduction, and label enhancement
  </Step>
  <Step title="Notification Delivery">
    Responders are notified according to the escalation rule, with multi-level escalation support
  </Step>
  <Step title="Incident Handling">
    Responders acknowledge, handle, and close incidents to complete the loop
  </Step>
</Steps>
